What Inspired the Project

The average time spent on social media in a day by an adult was two and a half hours in 2019, but the social media differ from person to an other. Every year a new social media goes trending.

So we came with the idea to group them in one place, CrossMe, where you will find your friend who's not using the social media that you are using.
